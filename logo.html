Logo

For this assignment I followed up to Logo tutorial . First, I created regalia and scaled it to the right size, then I added two ribbons and colored them. Also I used rectangle and star tool to create centerpiece shape, and applied gradient color on it. After this, I droped shadows to make logo more realistic.
Last step was to create a text. I did not have Lobster font in my library, so I needed to instal it to be closer to the tutorial. It was interesting tutorial for me, I got new skills for my future project in Adobe Illustrator.
