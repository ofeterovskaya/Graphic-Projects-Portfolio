Humorous E-Card

For this assignment, I created a humorous e-Card using at least three RAW images that were provided, I used four. I put together two identical images . I put together two identical images and flip horizontally one of them, created the background for my composition.

Then, I added photos of a "groom" Then, I added photos of a "bride" Then, I added

I used masks to non-destructively remove the background. After resizing images and put them together, I changed the brightness, levels, and hue of several of the layers to make them look like whole picture. Additionally, I changed the curves, overall brightness and contrast of the composition. I added a warm photo filter to the composition to make warmer colors. Also, I used blur effect, to make cars on the background not in the focus, and I applied liquify filter to make "groom's" and "bride's" smiles
