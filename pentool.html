Adobe Illustrator. Pen Tool

For this assignment, I picked 3 images related to Spring theme: flower, butterfly, and kite. It is my first experience with Adobe Illustrator. I used Pen Tool to outline the images, used curves, and anchor point as well. At the end, I filled up the figures by color.

Adding more color texture, and some details to these shadows, I could create some logos form these outlined images.
