About Me

The web design is an adventure for me. The adventure where I can create something new for you and at the same time to get emotional gratification. When I design, I am thinking about the audience that will be using it. I try to choose elements that support a message. I avoid unnecessary elements that might distract from the target. I add only necessary elements to create a mood, yet each element is chosen because it contributes to the concept of the work. Every work is unique, I create new Web Pages using CSS, HTML, Dreamweaver, and Java Script, also I like to work with Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator, and Adobe Animate. It is a pleasure for me to create projects for you. I love listening people and work with them to converting their plans and ideas to the reality. I put my attention into every single detail, and your dreams come true! Also my hobby are interior design and digital photography . I am trying to help my friends to decorate their homes, rooms, especially with curtains which help to make space more cozy. I am trying to be in the trend, so I am reading news about interior design , visit exhibitions and have fun time with my family.
