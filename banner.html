North Carolina Mountains Banner

For this assignment I created banner ad size:728px:90px in Adobe Illustrator. This banner should attract middled aged visitors to the North Carolina Mountains during the fall season. I used for the background, I reduced opacity for this image, applied outer glow and rounded corner effects. I used this image to attract couples, to show more romantic place in the mountains.
