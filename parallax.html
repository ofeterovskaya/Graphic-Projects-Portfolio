Parallax-Effect.

Doing this assignment, I have learned how to do Parallax Effect Photo. I picked a photo from Jordan Lake, because there is a sail boat there and I think that moving sail boat is a great idea to add a little bit movement to this picture. Using pen tool I outlined sailboat, and added it to the new layer. Then I modified the layer, and removed outlined figure from the picture. The next step was to create new document with video dimension: 1920*1080px , and moved my layers on it. After this, I convert every layer to the Smart Object, and created Timeline video where zooming in and zooming out the layers, I transformed them. I believe, that the movement, I added to the photo is very organic and smooth. The sailboat is bigger on the foreground, moving away, get smaller.
