Surreal Composite.

For this assignment I needed to pick at least three images and to create Dali-inspired composition. I tried to apply all my photoshop skills I learned during this semester so far. I picked a sky background, a clown from McDonalds, a tube, a clock, and a cow. I used warp element to squeeze the tube into the clown's pocket, applied mask to remove unnecessary tube's part, changed tube's color and added some color effects on it. Then, I also applied warp effect to squeeze the cow from the tube. Then, I made some copies of the cow's layer and applied different filters for each cow, adding blur effect for some of them, and transforming and resizing them making flying effect. In addition, I made mask layer for some cows and selected light lightning effect, so the cows look like clouds. Finally, I picked perspective and warp effect for the clock, and using mask changed the clock's color.

An Idea of this composition could be: "While you are enjoying McDonalds, time flies, and more and more cows are flying away."
